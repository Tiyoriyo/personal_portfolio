about me

I'm a Russian born, Dubai bred, and British taught web developer that has been trying to improve his craft since the beginning of 2022.

I have always wanted to learn how to code ever since I was a little kid. I loved playing games, so one of my dreams was to make one of my own, but the problem was that I didn't have the work ethic as a kid to learn the languages needed to create my dream into a reality.

Fast forward more than a decade and a half later, I was talking with a friend of mine on Discord, and we were reminiscing the days when we used to dream of coding, and both of us agreed that it was a shame we never attempted to fulfill that dream of ours. I guess right after that we both decided to learn, and try to make a career out of it, and so here I am, currently self-studying Javascript, specifically the React framework through the odin project.
